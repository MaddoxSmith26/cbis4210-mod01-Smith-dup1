My Study Abroad Adventure

During my International Design Thinking and AI study abroad program (May 11 - June 1, 2025), I embarked on an incredible journey across Ireland, Germany, and France. This transformative experience combined cutting-edge AI training, international collaboration, and cultural immersion that shaped my perspective on global business and technology.

Ireland

AI Development Training: Participated in hands-on AI development training with Orcawise in Dublin. Learned cutting-edge AI technologies and their practical applications in business environments.

Key experiences: AI literacy & governance training, prompt engineering, and exploring Dublin's vibrant tech scene.

Germany

SAP & Design Thinking: Collaborated with University of Muenster MIS students on a design thinking project for Viadee, a German IT consulting company. Visited SAP headquarters in Walldorf to learn about digital innovations.

Key experiences: International teamwork, business process analysis, and exposure to enterprise software solutions at SAP.

France

Cultural Immersion: Extended my European experience to explore French culture, art, and business practices. This cultural immersion complemented my technical learning with broader European perspectives.

Key experiences: European business culture, art and architecture appreciation, and understanding global market dynamics.
