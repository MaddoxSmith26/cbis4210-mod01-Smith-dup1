France - Cultural Tourism & Parisian Adventures

Two and a Half Days in Paris

Unlike Germany and Ireland where we focused on business and academics, France was pure cultural exploration. We spent our time discovering the beauty, history, and excitement of Paris - from world-famous landmarks to celebrating with locals as Paris won the Champions League!

Day 1: Seine River Dinner Cruise

Started our Parisian adventure with an enchanting dinner cruise along the Seine River. As we dined on French cuisine, we watched the City of Light illuminate before our eyes, seeing iconic landmarks from a unique perspective as they reflected in the river's waters.

Highlights: French cuisine, illuminated landmarks, Seine River views
Day 2: Art & Architecture

Immersed ourselves in world-class art at the Louvre, home to the Mona Lisa and countless masterpieces. Later explored the opulent Palace of Versailles with its stunning gardens and Hall of Mirrors. Ended the day ascending to the top of the Eiffel Tower for breathtaking panoramic views of Paris.

Highlights: Louvre masterpieces, Versailles palace, Eiffel Tower summit
Day 3: Historic Monuments & Celebration

Explored Paris's most famous monuments including majestic cathedrals, the iconic Arc de Triomphe, and Napoleon's Tomb at Les Invalides. The day became even more special when we joined locals celebrating Paris Saint-Germain winning the Champions League - experiencing the city's passion firsthand!

Highlights: Cathedrals, Arc de Triomphe, Napoleon's Tomb, Champions League celebration
